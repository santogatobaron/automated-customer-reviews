A functional and affordable CD binder with questionable design choices.

Users praise the binder's ability to store a large number of CDs in a compact space. Some buyers express disappointment with the material quality and design flaws, particularly the inability of the pages to sit flat when filled with discs.

Key Features

  • Large capacity: Holds up to 400 CDs in 50 pages with 8 pockets per page.
  • Affordable price: Offers good value for the storage provided.
  • Netting trim: Adds a refined appearance compared to plain binders.

Pros

  • Effective use of space for CD storage
  • Attractive netting trim finish
  • Competitive price point

Cons

  • Pages cannot sit flat when filled with discs
  • Plastic film may tear due to tension on the spine
  • Inconvenient for browsing due to bent pages

Final Assessment

This CD binder offers a functional and budget-friendly solution for storing a large collection of CDs. However, its design flaws, such as the inability of the pages to sit flat and the potential for the plastic film to tear, may detract from its overall value for some users.
